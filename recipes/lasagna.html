Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish consisting of layers of stacked lasagne alternating with other fillings. These layers could consist of many other things such as ground meat with tomato sauce, vegetables, various types of cheeses, seasonings and spices and more.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodle
  • lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper
  • tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • shredded mozzarella
  • ricotta cheese
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
